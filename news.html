Recommended News & Periodical Sources

A curated list top foster a discerning, well-rounded media diet.

News Outlets

Al Jazeera English

Global South perspective with bold investigative journalism offering unique insights into international affairs.

Channel NewsAsia

Balanced, measured reportage with a strong Asia-Pacific focus and comprehensive regional coverage.

The Hindu

India's leading national newspaper providing comprehensive South Asia coverage and analysis.

The Guardian

Thoughtful editorial voice with award-winning investigative journalism and opinion analysis.

BBC News

Broad international coverage with high editorial standards and in-depth analysis sections.

Reuters

A globally respected news agency offering timely, impartial, and free access to international news and financial markets coverage.

Scientific & Academic

Nature

Premier scientific journal with authoritative coverage of cutting-edge research across all scientific disciplines.

Science Magazine

Leading journal publishing significant scientific discoveries across a broad spectrum of disciplines.

Scientific American

Accessible yet precise science and technology coverage, making complex research understandable.

Physics World

Specialized focus on physics news, research breakthroughs, and developments in the field.

Quanta Magazine

Rigorous, artfully written explorations at the frontiers of mathematics, theoretical physics, biology, and computer science—free from hype, rich in substance.

The Public Domain Review

A distinguished online journal dedicated to the celebration and exploration of cultural and historical treasures in the public domain, offering erudite essays on forgotten manuscripts, art, and literature—free and accessible to all.

Intellectual & Cultural Commentary

The Conversation

Free, expert-written essays and analyses on global current affairs, science, and the humanities, authored by scholars and academics.

The Atlantic

Long-form journalism expertly blending politics, science, technology, and society.

Aeon

Philosophy, culture, and big ideas explored with remarkable intellectual depth and clarity.
