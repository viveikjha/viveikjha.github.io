Welcome to the Physics Refresher Course

This comprehensive course is designed to provide a thorough review of fundamental physics concepts, from classical mechanics to modern astrophysics. Whether you're a student looking to strengthen your understanding, a professional seeking to refresh your knowledge, or simply a curious mind exploring the laws that govern our universe, this course offers detailed explanations, interactive elements, and practical problems to deepen your comprehension.

Each topic is presented with a focus on conceptual understanding, mathematical formulation, and practical application. The course is structured to build progressively, with each subject connecting to others in a cohesive framework that highlights the unity of physical principles across different domains.

Explore the topics below to begin your journey through the fascinating world of physics, from the motion of everyday objects to the evolution of galaxies and the quantum nature of reality.
